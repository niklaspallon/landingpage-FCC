About the Game

In Fantasy Crosscountry, you build your own team of top cross-country skiers. Each athlete has a price based on performance, and you have a limited budget. The better your team performs in real-world competitions, the more points you earn.

Point System

Points are awarded based on race results, with bonuses for things like stage wins, strong climbs, or personal bests.

Team Building

Strategy matters! Balance well-known stars with promising talents to optimize your score.

Rules & Tips

Basic Rules

  • 6 skiers per team
  • Total budget: 100 million $
  • 3 athletes of each gender
  • Team changes allowed before each race weekend

Tips for Beginners

  • Track recent form and performance
  • Mix sprinters and distance specialists
  • Watch for injuries or DNS

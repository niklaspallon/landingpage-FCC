Rules and FAQ

Everything you need to assemble a balanced team, time your transfers, and stay on top of scoring all season long.

Team Building

Squad Size

  • Each manager selects six skiers for their squad.
  • The roster must include three women and three men.

Budget

  • Managers start with a 100 million $ budget.
  • Prices track real-world form, results history, and projected potential.
  • Price changes occur at the start of each new gameweek.

Balance & Strategy

  • Blend sprinters, distance specialists, and reliable all-rounders.
  • Cover individual races, stage events, and relay opportunities.

Transfers & Squad Management

Before the Weekend

  • Unlimited transfers are allowed until 30 minutes before the first start.
  • Moves made after the deadline take effect the following World Cup weekend.

Planning

  • Unused transfers do not roll over - maximize each round's potential.
  • Monitor injury news and team selections to avoid DNS surprises.
  • Each manager receives one free transfer when a new gameweek opens; if the upcoming schedule includes relay or team sprint events you receive two free transfers.
  • Additional transfers beyond the free allocation cost 40 points each.

Form & Price Changes

  • Skier prices shift dynamically based on recent results.
  • Market swings can open value gaps - stay alert on race weeks.

Scoring System

Base Points

Individual races reward podium finishes heavily and continue down to 1 point for 30th place, while relay and team sprint events have a separate scale capped at 15th place.

Absences

  • DNS or DSQ results award zero points for that weekend.
  • Injury flags inside the game warn you before lock deadlines.

Captain Boost

  • Select one captain each week; their total score for that round is doubled.
  • If the captain does not start, no double applies and the score remains zero.

Deadlines & Results

Deadlines

  • Transfers lock 30 minutes ahead of the first race start.
  • Time zones adjust automatically to your profile preference.

Adjustments

  • Any FIS corrections sync to your account within 24 hours.
  • A full change log stays available for complete transparency.

FAQ

What happens if a race is cancelled?

Cancelled races return zero points. If organizers reschedule, your current squads and transfers carry over automatically.

How do leagues work?

Create private leagues or join open ones. Points count for each gameweek and across the full season table.

Where can I get support?

Reach us via the contact form and we will respond as soon as we can.
